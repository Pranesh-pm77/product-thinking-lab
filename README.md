# product-thinking-lab

This repository documents my journey toward becoming a Product Manager
through hands-on product case studies based on real-world problems.

## Why this repository exists
- I believe product management is about problem-solving, not certificates
- I learn by analyzing real problems and designing practical solutions
- This repository is my proof of thinking, not theory

## What you’ll find here
- Product case studies on education, student life, and early-career problems
- Clear problem definitions, proposed solutions, MVPs, and metrics
- Structured thinking that improves with every case study

## Current Focus
- Student problems in India
- Education, admissions, and placement systems
- Simple, scalable product ideas

## Long-Term Goal
To become a Product Manager who builds meaningful, user-focused products.
