# Case Study 1: College Admission Confusion in India

## User
Class 12 students from tier-2 and tier-3 cities in India,
especially first-generation college applicants.

## Problem
Students struggle to understand college options, eligibility criteria,
and application deadlines. This results in missed opportunities,
stress, and poor decision-making.

## Current Solutions
- YouTube videos
- College websites
- Admission agents
- Telegram and WhatsApp groups

## Gaps in Current Solutions
- Information overload
- Outdated or biased advice
- No personalization
- Low trust in agents

## Proposed Solution
A centralized, personalized admission guidance platform that helps
students track deadlines, eligibility, and suitable colleges based
on their academic profile and budget.

## MVP
- Google Form to collect student data
- Google Sheet with verified deadlines
- Notion-based dashboard for recommendations

## Success Metrics
- Reduction in missed deadlines
- Time saved per student
- Repeat usage rate

## Risks
- Data accuracy issues
- Scalability challenges
- Competition from existing platforms
